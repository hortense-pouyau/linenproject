1. OUR PROJECT: why a 100% linen brand?

Linen is a local and renewable resource, and France is the world's leading producer. It is a GMO-free crop, and is grown in crop rotation, thus encouraging the natural biological activity of the soil. It requires very little irrigation, and a minimal amount of fertiliser and pesticides, and is often close to the organic standard. Linen is naturally hypoallergenic, thermoregulating and very resistant.
